About Ryan

I was born in New Zealand and spent most of my childhood living in Invercargill. When I was 15 I moved to the Sunshine Coast and completed the last 2 years of school with the same Benjamin that is part of this team. From here I completed first year Auto Electrical at Tafe and then went on to work for about 5 years in the same trade. Prompted by a lull in the industry I moved down to a small country town North West of Ipswich call Lowood. I spent the next 10 years as a Traffic controller in the construction industry, until I followed my heart and landed my current Job at Apple inc. I have been with Apple for almost 5 years and would like to move in to software devlopment.
